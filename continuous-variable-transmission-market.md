<p><strong>The Continuous Variable Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Connection</li><li>Chain Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT) market can be segmented into two primary types based on connection methods: belt and chain connection systems. Belt-connected CVTs utilize flexible belts to adjust the transmission ratio seamlessly, offering smooth acceleration and enhanced fuel efficiency, commonly found in smaller vehicles. In contrast, chain-connected CVTs employ durable chains, providing robust performance and better torque handling, suitable for heavier vehicles and applications needing high reliability. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements, influencing consumer choice in the automotive market.</p></p>
