<p><ul><li>Audio A/D Converters</li><li>Audio D/A Converters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio DAC market consists of two primary types: audio A/D converters and audio D/A converters. Audio A/D converters transform analog audio signals into digital format, enabling the capture of sound for various applications, including recording and broadcasting. Conversely, audio D/A converters convert digital audio signals back into analog form, facilitating playback on speakers or headphones. Both types are essential in the audio chain, ensuring high-quality sound reproduction and seamless integration between analog and digital audio systems across various industries.</p></p>
