<p><p>Active Battery Cell Balancing is a technique used in battery management systems to optimize the performance and longevity of batteries by equalizing the charge levels of individual cells. Unlike passive balancing, which dissipates excess energy as heat, active balancing redistributes energy from more charged cells to less charged ones, enhancing overall efficiency and extending battery lifespan.</p><p>The Active Battery Cell Balancing Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for reliable energy storage solutions in electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, and consumer electronics.
