<p><p>The Door Access Control Solution market comprises three primary types: card-based, biometrics-based, and others. Card-based systems utilize key cards or fobs for entry, offering convenience and flexibility, often integrated with various technologies like RFID. Biometrics-based solutions employ unique personal identifiers such as fingerprints, facial recognition, or iris scans, enhancing security and reducing the risk of unauthorized access. The "others" category encompasses innovations like keypads, mobile access, and mobile apps, providing diverse options to cater to varying security needs.</p></p>
