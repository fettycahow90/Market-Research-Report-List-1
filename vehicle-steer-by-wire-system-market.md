<p><p>The steer-by-wire system market encompasses several key components. The Electronic Control Unit (ECU) processes inputs from the driver and controls the system. Road wheel actuators translate the ECU commands into physical movements of the wheels. Force feedback actuators provide sensory feedback, enhancing driver engagement by simulating the feel of traditional steering. Steering motors facilitate the movement of the steering mechanism, while angular sensors monitor the steering angle, ensuring precise control and vehicle responsiveness. Together, they enhance safety and driving experience.</p></p>
