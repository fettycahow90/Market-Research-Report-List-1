<p><strong>The RFID Pet Microchips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>125 kHz Microchip</li><li>128 kHz Microchip</li><li>134.2 kHz Microchip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID pet microchips are categorized into three main types based on their frequency: 125 kHz, 128 kHz, and 134.2 kHz. The 125 kHz microchip, often older technology, offers basic identification. The 128 kHz microchip provides enhanced range and performance, suitable for various applications. The 134.2 kHz microchip, widely adopted in pet identification globally, adheres to international standards, ensuring compatibility with scanners and improving the chances of lost pets being recovered efficiently through microchip databases.</p></p>
