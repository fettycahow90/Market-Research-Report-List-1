<p><ul><li>Passive Electronic Filter</li><li>Active Electronic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-pass electronic filter market comprises two main types: passive and active filters. Passive electronic filters rely on passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to remove low-frequency signals without requiring external power. In contrast, active electronic filters utilize amplifiers and active components to enhance performance, allowing for better control over frequency response and providing gain. Both types play crucial roles in communications, audio processing, and various electronic applications, each offering unique advantages tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
