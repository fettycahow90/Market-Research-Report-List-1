<p><ul><li>Flange Type</li><li>Welding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The externally pressurized expansion joints market comprises two primary types: flange type and welding type. Flange type joints feature flanges at both ends, allowing for easy installation and connection to piping systems, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, welding type joints are directly welded to the piping, offering enhanced durability and leak resistance. Both types effectively accommodate thermal expansion, vibration, and movement in various industrial settings, ensuring system integrity and performance across demanding environments.</p></p>
