<p><p>Oriented Strand Board (OSB) sheathing is categorized into four main types based on load-bearing capabilities and moisture resistance. OSB/1 is designed for internal dry areas, while OSB/2 is suitable for use in dry construction scenarios that require moderate strength. OSB/3 is engineered for load-bearing applications in humid conditions, offering enhanced moisture resistance. Lastly, OSB/4 is intended for use in applications with high moisture levels, delivering superior structural integrity and durability in challenging environments. Each type serves specific construction needs.</p></p>
