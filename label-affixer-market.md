<p><p>Label affixer machines are categorized into semi-automatic and automatic types based on operational efficiency and user involvement. Semi-automatic machines require manual feeding of products and positioning for label application, making them suitable for small-scale operations with lower budgets. In contrast, automatic label affixers fully mechanize the process, automatically feeding products, applying labels, and often offering higher speeds and accuracy, ideal for large-scale production environments that demand efficiency and minimal labor intervention.</p></p>
