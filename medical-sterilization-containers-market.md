<p><p>The medical sterilization containers market is categorized into two main types: filter type and valve type. Filter type containers utilize integrated filters that allow steam or gases to circulate while preventing contamination, ensuring effective sterilization. Valve type containers feature special valves that maintain internal pressure and facilitate steam entry and exit, enhancing sterilization efficiency. Both types are designed for safe transport and storage of medical instruments, ensuring patient safety and compliance with healthcare standards throughout the sterilization process.</p></p>
