<p><p>The gel electrophoresis equipment market is primarily categorized into horizontal and vertical designs. Horizontal systems are typically used for separating nucleic acids, providing a flat gel surface that allows for even migration. They are popular in applications like DNA analysis and paternity testing. In contrast, vertical systems are suited for protein separation, allowing for better resolution due to a tighter slab gel environment. This distinction caters to the specific needs of various biological research and diagnostic applications, enhancing overall efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
