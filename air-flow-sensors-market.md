<p><strong>The Air Flow Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass Air Flow Sensors</li><li>Volume Air Flow Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air flow sensors market is primarily classified into two types: mass air flow (MAF) sensors and volume air flow (VAF) sensors. MAF sensors measure the mass of air entering an engine, enabling precise fuel injection and optimizing performance and emissions. In contrast, VAF sensors quantify the volume of air, often used in HVAC systems to regulate airflow and maintain comfort levels. Both sensor types play critical roles in enhancing engine efficiency and ensuring effective air management in various applications.</p></p>
