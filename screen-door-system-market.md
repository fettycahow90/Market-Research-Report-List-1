<p><ul><li>Full Height</li><li>Half Height</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screen door system market is divided into two main types: full height and half height. Full height screen doors span the entire height of the entrance, providing maximum protection against insects and allowing for unobstructed views. They are ideal for larger openings and often used in residential and commercial settings. Half height screen doors, conversely, cover only a portion of the entryway, balancing airflow and visibility while serving as an economical option for lighter traffic areas. Both types cater to varying customer needs and preferences.</p></p>
