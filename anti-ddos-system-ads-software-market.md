<p><p>The Anti-DDoS System (ADS) software market is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based ADS offers scalability and flexibility, allowing businesses to leverage remote servers for enhanced security against distributed denial-of-service attacks. This model typically features automatic updates and global protection. In contrast, on-premise ADS is installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, providing direct control and customization of security measures but may require significant maintenance and resource investment. Both options aim to ensure robust protection against DDoS threats.</p></p>
