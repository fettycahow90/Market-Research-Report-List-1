<p><p>Natural gas tankless water heaters are efficient heating systems that provide hot water on demand without the need for a storage tank. Unlike traditional water heaters, which maintain a constant supply of hot water, tankless systems heat water directly as it flows through the unit, leading to significant energy savings and smaller footprints.
